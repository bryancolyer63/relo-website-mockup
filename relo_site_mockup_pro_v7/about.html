About Relo Insurance

Relo Insurance was built to support one of the most overlooked parts of employee relocation.

Why we exist

We created Relo Insurance because insurance is part of relocation, whether the industry treats it that way or not.

A work move changes housing, vehicles, timing, coverage needs, and peace of mind. Insurance should be handled with that context in mind.

Our role is simple

Help relocating employees navigate insurance with less stress while supporting the broader relocation experience for partners and stakeholders.

  • Bring more focus to a real gap in relocation
  • Support people during one of life’s biggest transitions
  • Help partners deliver a more complete employee experience

Our point of view

Relocation is about helping people land well.

Insurance may not be the most visible part of that journey, but it matters. When it is handled well, people feel supported. When it is not, friction shows up fast.

What we believe

The relocation industry has an opportunity to better support employees during one of the biggest transitions in their lives.

Relo Insurance exists to help make that possible.
