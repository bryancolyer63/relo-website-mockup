Who we serve

Relo Insurance is built for the companies moving employees, the partners supporting the move, and the employees living through it.

Talk with our teamExplore partnerships

For corporates

Relocation is already a high-stakes employee moment. Insurance should not become another loose end.

Relo Insurance helps support employees through that part of the process so the relocation experience feels more complete, more thoughtful, and better aligned with what employees actually face.

For relocation management companies

Insurance is one of the least visible gaps in relocation and one of the easiest places for stress to show up.

Relo Insurance helps fill that gap with a specialized approach built around relocation.

For destination services providers

Insurance often sits just outside destination support, even though it affects readiness and peace of mind.

Relo Insurance complements that journey with focused insurance support for relocating employees.

For transferees

A work move creates enough decisions already. Insurance should be easier to handle.

Relo Insurance helps relocating employees navigate home, auto, and renters insurance with more clarity and less friction.
